# Sprint 1 — Cast And Institution Logic

## Specs Covered

- `docs/_specs/curatorial-enrichment/spec.md`

## Goal

Make the people and studios surface behave more like a cast page than a flat directory.

## Scope

### In

- reorganize people and institutions by historical function where needed
- make the relationship between cast entries and chronology visible
- strengthen interpretive language so the page reads as museum interpretation
- update the collection model if the cast logic needs supporting guidance

### Out

- turning the page into a full encyclopedia or exhaustive directory
- adding unrelated new rooms or media systems
- changing chronology pages except where cross-linking is required

## Tasks

1. Reorganize the cast logic if needed.
   - group people and institutions by historical function rather than by simple list order
   - make the relationship to chronology visible
2. Improve interpretive language.
   - show why each person or institution matters to a room or turning point
   - reduce directory-style writing

## Files to touch

- `docs/people-and-studios.html`
- `docs/content/collection-model.md` if supporting guidance is needed

## Acceptance criteria

- cast group logic is historically meaningful
- the page supports chronology rather than competing with it
- interpretive language is stronger and less directory-like
- people and institutions feel connected to the exhibition narrative
