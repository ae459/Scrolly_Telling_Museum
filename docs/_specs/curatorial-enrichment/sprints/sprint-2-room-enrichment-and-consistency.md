# Sprint 2 — Room Enrichment And Consistency

## Specs Covered

- `docs/_specs/curatorial-enrichment/spec.md`

## Goal

Improve room-level evidence and then review the whole exhibition for consistency.

## Scope

### In

- strengthen weak rooms with targeted object or caption improvements
- perform a cross-room consistency review after enrichment
- protect chronology as the spine of the exhibition
- avoid decorative or redundant additions

### Out

- large-scale expansion that overloads the exhibition
- redesigning the site outside evidence and consistency needs
- changing unrelated documentation that does not support room enrichment

## Tasks

1. Add or sharpen room evidence where justified.
   - strengthen weak rooms with targeted object or caption improvements
   - avoid decorative additions
2. Perform cross-room consistency review.
   - confirm rooms feel related but not repetitive
   - confirm the chronology remains the spine

## Files to touch

- relevant pages in `docs/`

## Acceptance criteria

- room evidence is stronger where changes were made
- no room feels overloaded or redundant
- cross-room consistency is improved
- chronology remains the organizing spine of the exhibition

## Verification checklist

- [ ] run HTML/CSS/SVG error review on touched files
- [ ] inspect the updated routes in browser preview
- [ ] verify no added evidence feels decorative or repetitive
- [ ] verify room relationships still read as one exhibition system
